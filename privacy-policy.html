Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit jasminchacko.com, how it is used, and how it is protected. This site is operated by Jasmin Chacko, BCBA.

Important: Please do not send any protected health information (PHI), client details, or sensitive personal information through the contact form on this website. This site is not a secure clinical communication platform and is not HIPAA-compliant for the transmission of PHI.

1. Information Collected

This website collects only the information you voluntarily provide through the contact form:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message

No other personal data is collected. This website does not currently use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools.

2. How Your Information Is Used

The information you submit through the contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or question
  • Follow up on a consultation or collaboration request

Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

This website uses Formspree to process contact form submissions. When you submit the contact form, your name, email, and message are transmitted to Formspree's servers and then forwarded to this site's owner. Formspree's own privacy policy governs how they handle that data. You can review it at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting this site's owner directly.

5. External Links

This site may contain links to external websites (such as podcast platforms or professional resources). This Privacy Policy applies only to jasminchacko.com. Jasmin Chacko is not responsible for the privacy practices of any linked third-party sites.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date at the top.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out through the contact form on this website.
